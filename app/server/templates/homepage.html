Performance Platform

Data and statistics to help government departments improve public services

The Performance Platform collects and publishes data to show how government services are being used. Departments can then act on this data to improve services for their users.

Helping departments measure services

To understand how government services are performing we measure four key performance indicators (KPIs). With these metrics, government departments can better see where improvements are needed.
