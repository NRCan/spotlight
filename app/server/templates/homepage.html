How departments measure services

To understand how government services are performing we measure four key performance indicators (KPIs). With these metrics, government departments can better see where improvements are needed.

Cost per transaction

The cost of running the service per person who uses it.

Application for passport

It costs £61 to administrate, produce and issue a passport.

Vehicle tax renewal

It costs £1.27 to process a renewal of a vehicle tax disc.

Marine licences

It costs £1,632 to assess, administer, print and post a marine licence.

User satisfaction

Satisfaction is measured by asking people who use the service a few questions when they complete a service.

Voter registration

People are 94% satisfied with the service when they register to vote.

Register a birth abroad

People are 82% satisfied with the service when they register a birth abroad online.

Practical driving test bookings

People are 85% satisfied when they book a practical driving test online.

Completion rate

The number of people who manage to successfully complete a government service once they have started it.
