How departments measure services

To understand how government services are performing we measure 4 metrics. With this data, government departments can see where improvements are needed.

Cost per transaction

What it costs government to run a service.

Renew a passport

It costs £61 to administrate, produce and issue a passport.

Vehicle tax renewal

It costs £1.27 to process vehicle tax renewal.

User satisfaction

Satisfaction is calculated by asking people to rate a service once they've finished using it.

Voter registration

The majority of people are very satisfied with the register to vote service.

Register a birth abroad

The majority of people are satisfied with the register a birth abroad service.

Completion rate

The number of people who manage to successfully complete a government service once they have started it.

Voter registration

People are 94% satisfied with the service when they register to vote.

Register a birth abroad

People are 82% satisfied with the service when they register a birth abroad online.

Digital take-up

The number of people using government services online.
